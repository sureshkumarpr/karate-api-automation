## 🎨 DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS

### **Color Scheme**
- **Primary**: Blue (#2E86AB) - Professional, trustworthy
- **Secondary**: Green (#A23B72) - Success, growth
- **Accent**: Orange (#F18F01) - Energy, innovation
- **Neutral**: Gray (#F5F5F5) - Clean background

### **Typography**
- **Title Font**: Calibri Light or Arial (32-44pt)
- **Heading Font**: Calibri or Arial (24-32pt)
- **Body Font**: Calibri or Arial (18-22pt)
- **Code Font**: Consolas or Courier New (14-18pt)

### **Layout Guidelines**
- **Consistent Headers**: Same style throughout
- **White Space**: Don't overcrowd slides
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Important info larger/prominent
- **Alignment**: Consistent left alignment or centered

### **Visual Elements**
- **Icons**: Use consistent icon style (Fluent UI or Material)
- **Charts**: Use PowerPoint built-in charts
- **Diagrams**: Use SmartArt for professional look
- **Images**: High-quality, relevant images

## ⏰ TIMING RECOMMENDATIONS

### **Total Presentation Time**: 30-40 minutes

#### **Slide Timing Distribution**
- **Slides 1-3**: 3 minutes (Introduction)
- **Slides 4-9**: 10 minutes (Core Modules)
- **Slides 10-12**: 5 minutes (Performance & CI/CD)
- **Slides 13-15**: 5 minutes (Environment & Demo)
- **Slides 16-19**: 5 minutes (Academic & Results)
- **Slides 20-21**: 5 minutes (Conclusion & Q&A)

#### **Practice Tips**
- **Rehearse**: Practice full presentation multiple times
- **Timing**: Use timer to stay on schedule
- **Transitions**: Plan smooth transitions between topics
- **Questions**: Prepare for potential questions

## 🎯 PRESENTATION TIPS

### **Before Presentation**
- ✅ **Check Equipment**: Projector, clicker, microphone
- ✅ **Test Slides**: Ensure all elements work correctly
- ✅ **Backup**: Have PDF backup and notes
- ✅ **Practice**: Full rehearsal with timing

### **During Presentation**
- ✅ **Eye Contact**: Engage with audience
- ✅ **Speak Clearly**: Moderate pace, clear articulation
- ✅ **Use Notes**: Reference but don't read
- ✅ **Handle Questions**: Prepare for common questions

### **After Presentation**
- ✅ **Thank Audience**: Professional closing
- ✅ **Share Resources**: Provide links/references
- ✅ **Collect Feedback**: For improvement
- ✅ **Follow Up**: Answer additional questions
